# Code of Conduct

We follow the Contributor Covenant (v2.1).

- Be respectful, inclusive, and constructive.
- Assume good intent; disagree without being disagreeable.
- Do not harass or discriminate against others.
- Keep discussions professional; no spam or disruptive behavior.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers with relevant details. Consequences may include warnings, temporary bans, or removal from the community spaces.

For the full text, see https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html.
